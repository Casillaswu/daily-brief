This Week At A Glance

Three things in PH iGaming this week: PT Gaming officially launched, completing PlayTime's group-brand transition; BingoPlus Night opens in 15 days, with a Manila Times full-page ad on 5/4 kicking off the media push; Mother's Day and payday — none of the 9 brands have any campaign tied to either.

1. PT Gaming (PlayTime's casino product) is now live.
Last week ptgaming.ph was a placeholder. This week it has a complete page: "PAGCOR Licensed / For All Your Gaming Needs," with 9 supplier logos at the bottom (JILI, Pragmatic Play, Evolution, PG, FC, JDB, SA Gaming, Pinnacle, Realtime Gaming). playtime.ph now shows a modal: "PLAYTIME ENTERTAINMENT will be the future group page; for PT Gaming please visit ptgaming.ph." playtime.fit has been rebuilt as a full SEO landing site (full product menu + Vic Sotto endorser copy + ₱7,730 welcome bonus + "10M+ Filipino players" narrative). (Source: ptgaming.ph + playtime.ph + playtime.fit site recon 5/4)

2. BingoPlus Night opens 5/19 — the media push begins this week.
Manila Times ran a full-page ad on 5/4. Same day, SAKSI Ngayon, Tribune, and BusinessMirror published the same syndicated newswire. The 10-act roster is locked (Vice Ganda, Kim Chiu, Ely Buendia, Bamboo, Parokya Ni Edgar, Nina, Julie Anne San Jose, Yeng Constantino, Darren Espanto, Anthony Jennings) plus 3 hosts (Joy Barcoma, Markki Stroem, Rufa Mae Quinto). GTV 5/22 8 PM and GMA 5/23 10 AM are both confirmed for rebroadcast. (Source: Manila Times 5/4)

3. Mother's Day 5/10 + payday 5/15 — none of the 9 brands have a targeted campaign.
CasinoPlus is still pushing 2.6% cashback and the jackpot board (left-side menu has a "2.6%Cashback" entry); no Mother's Day touch. Bet88 keeps its Chinese UI + Maya / Lucky Games / G-Life shared-account promo stack; no Mother's Day touch. King + Queen show recovered login/registration on the surface, but logged-in users still hit the maintenance page. Amusnet × 747Live enters week 2 of market entry. (Source: casinoplus.com.ph + bet88.ph + king.ph + queenking.ph site recon 5/4)

Strategic Read · 5 Questions

Q1
Market space

Three supply-side movements this week: PT Gaming launched with 9 suppliers, Amusnet × 747Live entered week 2 of market entry, OKGames brought multiple new campaigns live at once.

None of the 9 brands have any campaign tied to Mother's Day 5/10 or payday 5/15.

Source: W19 site recon 5/4 + 9-brand public-action audit
Q2
What players care about

Withdrawal speed, support response, KYC flow remain the top three concerns for PH players.

King + Queen are surface-recovered but still in maintenance after login. Players with balances on either site will be watching for "can I get my money out" this week.

Source: PH player community common topics + king.ph / queenking.ph site recon 5/4
Q3
Each brand's angle

BingoPlus runs celebrities + galas; ArenaPlus runs sports IP; CasinoPlus runs jackpot + cashback; Bet88 runs promo stack + Chinese UI; GameZone runs human-vs-human card games; OKGames runs endorser + multi-campaign; PlayTime / PT Gaming runs multi-supplier library.

Across all 9 angles, "festival theme" and "emotional storytelling" are the two quadrants no one is touching this week.

Source: this week's public actions + site recon 5/4 cross-check
Q4
Pricing

CasinoPlus offers 2.6% cashback; Bet88 offers 20 free spins + ₱8,888 first deposit; OKGames offers 60% Top-Up Rebate; PlayTime offers ₱7,730 welcome + 2.55% unlimited cashback; GameZone uses ₱12,888 daily tournaments to drive DAU.

The dollar amounts and structure are similar across brands; from a first-deposit-bonus standpoint, players have a hard time telling them apart.

Source: each brand's /promotions page, W19 site recon 5/4
Q5
Differentiation

None of the 9 brands have any campaign tied to Mother's Day this week.
