What's going on this week

Three things in PH iGaming this week. ArenaPlus 5/21 handed its sportsbook tech stack to Altenar (European vendor); BingoPlus Night 5/19 landed + twin broadcasts + Foundation ₱1B 3-year pledge; PAGCOR EGLD 5/21 memo: B2B suppliers must be PAGCOR-accredited by 7/31.

1. ArenaPlus 5/21 handed its sportsbook tech stack to Altenar (European sportsbook tech vendor, Sweden-listed, first entry into the PH regulated market). DigiPlus goes platform-driven on sports.
Altenar takes over the ArenaPlus betting engine — covering PBA player props, in-play live odds, automated pricing, risk management, and compliance modules. Combined with the April announcement that ArenaPlus is the NBA's first official betting partner in the Philippines, plus the existing Sportradar Integrity Exchange integration, DigiPlus now stacks sports as a "platform + IP + compliance" three-piece moat. Full event background, Client X impact, and reverse caveats in §04 card 1.(Source: AGB 5/21 · GGRAsia · Yogonet 5/22)

2. BingoPlus Night 5/19 landed + GTV 5/22 / GMA 5/23 twin broadcasts aired + DigiPlus Foundation publicly pledged ₱1B over 3 years.
₱100M prize pool (5/13-5/26); Vice Ganda + Kim Chiu + Ely Buendia + Bamboo + Parokya Ni Edgar lineup confirmed via public materials; the Foundation's 3-year ₱1B health/education/resilience commitment turns entertainment SOV into a second-stage "corporate social responsibility" narrative. Full event background, talent lineup, and reverse caveats in §04 card 2.(Source: NewsWatchPlus 5/21 · Manila Times 5/24)

3. PAGCOR EGLD (Electronic Gaming Licensing Department) 5/21 memo: B2B suppliers must be PAGCOR-accredited by 7/31 — otherwise they cannot continue to serve any PH PAGCOR-licensed operator.
Second hard rule following the 5/7 1.5% / 15% cap memo. GLI (the first PAGCOR-accredited ITL Independent Testing Laboratory, granted in March) was reported by IAG on 5/20 to be expanding its PH focus alongside the new framework. A supply-chain swap-out wave is expected to kick off in June-July. Full regulatory read and watchlist impact in §04 card 3.(Source: IAG 5/24 · Chambers and Partners)

Market space

Supply-side moves split two ways:

· Tech supply-chain swap-out pre-launches (ArenaPlus + Altenar template, see §04 card 1)
· Compliance-narrative differentiation window widens (post-5/15 dual wording + post-7/31 "full-stack accredited" narrative layer becomes possible, see §06 opportunity 1)

Key watch: if PAGCOR EGLD ships "ongoing rebate vs welcome frame" boundary guidance before end-June, the label-positioning strategy 10 brands rely on needs to be rebuilt overnight. 4-6 week observation window.

Card 3 · PAGCOR EGLD (Electronic Gaming Licensing Department)05/21 · Policy · Regulator
High

PAGCOR EGLD 5/21 memo: B2B suppliers must be PAGCOR-accredited by 7/31

Following the 5/7 1.5% / 15% cap memo, PAGCOR EGLD's 5/21 second hard rule: all unaccredited B2B suppliers (slot / casino / sports / live providers, affiliates, support services) must complete approval by 31 July 2026 — otherwise they cannot continue to serve any PH PAGCOR-licensed operator. GLI (Gaming Laboratories International — the first PAGCOR-accredited ITL Independent Testing Laboratory, granted in March 2026) was reported by IAG on 5/20 to be expanding its PH focus to support the new framework. What this means for the 10-brand watchlist: a supply-chain swap-out wave kicks off in June-July; brands relying on unaccredited suppliers face rapidly emerging operational interruption risk. Post-7/31, supplier accreditation status becomes a "player trust + regulatory compliance" dual-axis narrative asset. Zero brands have publicly disclosed their supplier accreditation status this week.
⚠️ Reverse caveat: 7/31 is PAGCOR's public deadline — it doesn't guarantee every B2B can complete in time (GLI launched testing services in early 2026 and hasn't disclosed average flow duration). If Client X plans to enter, every supplier in its stack must have verified accreditation status pre-entry.
Source: IAG 5/24 · IAG 5/20 GLI expansion · Chambers and Partners analysis
